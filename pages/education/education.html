Government Initiative

Beti Bachao Beti Padhao

A central government scheme that helps girls throughout the nation. The primary purpose is to save the child from social problems such as gender-based abortions and advance child education around the country.

  • Initially meant for districts with low sex ratio
  • Successfully extended to other regions
  • Educational programme to change societal attitudes
  • Focus on girl child protection and empowerment
Savings Scheme

Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana

A Government of India backed savings scheme designed for parents of girl children, encouraging systematic saving for education and marriage expenses.

  • Minimum deposit: ₹250 per annum
  • Maximum deposit: ₹1.5 lakhs
  • Tax exemption on deposits and interest
  • Account tenure: 21 years from opening
  • Partial withdrawal allowed at 18 years
  • Available at post offices and banks
Rajasthan Government

Mukhyamantri Rajshri Yojana

Launched in Rajasthan, offering monetary benefits to parents of girl children from birth till higher education.

  • ₹2,500 at birth of girl child
  • ₹2,500 at one year with vaccinations
  • ₹4,000 at Grade I admission
  • ₹5,000 at Grade VI
  • ₹11,000 at Grade XI
  • Only for permanent Rajasthan residents
  • Girls born after June 1, 2016 eligible
