Loan Facility

Mudra Loan

MUDRA stands for Micro Units Development & Refinance Agency Ltd. This loan is applicable to all self-employed and small business owners.

Loan Categories

  • Shishu: Covers loans up to ₹50,000 for startups and early-stage businesses
  • Kishore: Covers loans above ₹50,000 and up to ₹5 lakh for established businesses ready to scale
  • Tarun: Covers loans above ₹5 lakh and up to ₹10 lakh for well-established enterprises
  • Facilitates startups and enterprises to establish and expand their business
  • No collateral required for loans under this scheme
  • Available at all commercial banks, RRBs, small finance banks, and MFIs
SBI Scheme

Stree Shakti Package

Launched by State Bank of India (SBI) to promote entrepreneurship among women. This package provides financial assistance to women business owners with favorable terms.

  • Specifically designed for women entrepreneurs
  • Loan facility up to ₹2 crores under retail
  • 0.5% concession in interest rate for women borrowers
  • Additional 0.25% rate concession if CGTMSE/ECGC guarantee is availed
  • No processing fee required
  • Available for new and existing enterprises
  • Can be used for working capital or term loan requirements

Government Initiative

Udyogini Scheme

A comprehensive scheme established to financially aid women entrepreneurs, especially from economically weaker sections.

Eligibility & Benefits

  • Applicants must be between 18 and 45 years of age
  • Family income should be less than ₹40,000 per annum in urban areas
  • Family income should be less than ₹30,000 per annum in rural areas
  • Preference given to women from SC/ST and minority communities
  • Widows, destitute women, and physically handicapped women get priority
  • Loan amount ranges from ₹10,000 to ₹1 lakh
  • 30% subsidy on the loan amount provided by the government
  • Repayment period up to 10 years
