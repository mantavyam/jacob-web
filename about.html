Our Mission

Sabka Saath Sabka Vikaas

Women education awareness and support portal is a comprehensive platform helping India achieve its goal of "Sabka Saath Sabka Vikaas" (Together with All, Development for All) by empowering women through three core pillars:

Education

Enabling women to access better education through government schemes like Beti Bachao Beti Padhao and Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana.

Entrepreneurship

Supporting women entrepreneurs with financial schemes including Mudra Loan, Stree Shakti Yojana, and Udyogini Scheme.

Protection

Protecting women from social evils and gender-based violence through legal support and 24/7 helpline services.

Vision

An India Where Every Woman Thrives

We envision an India where every woman has equal access to education, economic opportunities, and lives free from violence and discrimination. Our vision is to create a society where women are empowered decision-makers, contributing equally to India's growth and development.

  • 100% female literacy
  • Equal entrepreneurship opportunities
  • Zero tolerance for gender-based violence
  • Women's economic independence
