Women Entrepreneurs

Schemes for Female Entrepreneurs

The increasing presence of women as entrepreneurs has led to significant business and economic growth in the country. Government of India is providing various incentives to promote female entrepreneurs for a sustainable tomorrow.

Business Schemes

Financial Support Programs

Mudra Loan for Women

Government scheme offering financial support for women entrepreneurs without collateral requirements.

Shishu Loan: Up to Rs. 50,000 for businesses in initial stage

Kishor Loan: Rs. 50,000 to Rs. 5 Lakhs for established businesses

Tarun Loan: Up to Rs. 10 Lakhs for business expansion

Stree Shakti Yojana

Unique government scheme supporting women entrepreneurship through concessions.

Eligibility: Women with majority ownership in business

Requirement: Enrollment under EDP organized by state agencies

Benefit: Interest concession of 0.05% on loans above Rs. 2 lakh

Udyogini Scheme

Program for women who want to establish their own business, especially from economically underdeveloped areas.

Target: Women from families earning less than Rs. 1.5 lakhs per year

Benefit: Up to Rs. 3 lakh with low-interest rates

Focus: No discrimination against widowed, poor, or disabled women
