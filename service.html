Empowering women through comprehensive support in education, entrepreneurship, and protection

Women's Education

Government schemes to improve literacy rates and empower women through education. Includes BBBP, Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana, and Mukhyamantri Rajshri Yojana.

  • Financial support for girl child education
  • Savings schemes for future
  • Scholarship programs
Learn More

Female Entrepreneurs

Financial support and incentives for women entrepreneurs. Programs include Mudra Loan, Stree Shakti Yojana, and Udyogini Scheme for sustainable business growth.

  • No collateral business loans
  • Interest rate concessions
  • Government-backed financial support
Explore

Protection Against Violence

Legal protection and support for women against gender-based violence. Know your rights under IPC and special laws. Access 24/7 helpline and complaint registration.

  • 24/7 helpline: 14490
  • Online complaint registration
  • Legal counseling and support
